Enter your name into the Player Name bar and place the ships labeled 1-5 onto the green grid.

You are able to double click the ships when on the grid and they will rotate if possible.

Understand that if the ship were to go out of bounds, over another ship, or fail to rotate the

ship willl not move from its last location.

When you are ready press Start.

Player Name Must Be At Least One Character Long!

All Ships Must Be Placed On The Grid!
